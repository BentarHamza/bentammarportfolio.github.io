ABOUT ME

I am Hamza, a seasoned BI Developer and Data Analyst with 4 years of hands-on experience. My core expertise lies in SQL and Python for intricate data analysis, bolstered by a solid foundation in DAX for measure creation in PowerBI and MDX for cube querying in SSAS. This proficiency is further complemented by my extensive experience with various database platforms including SQL Server, MySQL, PostgreSQL, and Oracle.

My professional trajectory has seen me take on diverse roles. As a Data Analyst in the construction consultancy sector, I delved deep into financial datasets, creating tools and interactive dashboards that provided pivotal financial insights. In an electromechanical equipment company, I played a pivotal role in centralizing KPIs, developing a Data Warehouse, integrating multi-source business data, and crafting insightful reports based on OLAP cubes. In a subsequent role with a major enterprise, I streamlined scattered Excel data into unified, interpretable KPI dashboards. Most recently, I had the unique opportunity to enhance the economic intelligence of a significant region, revamping their data collection and analysis processes.

Consistently across these roles, I've showcased my ability to engage with stakeholders, pinpoint specific data needs, design and put into action modern data architectures, and draw actionable insights through visualizations. I present a harmonious blend of technical depth and strategic vision, always striving for data-driven excellence. If you want to discuss my portfolio projects, Don't hesitate to reach out to me.
